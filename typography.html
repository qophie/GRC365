Typography Study

Once upon a time a fox saw a crow fly off with a piece of cheese in its beak and settle on a branch of a tree. The fox immediately wanted the cheese and thought of a clever way to get it.

“Flattery will get you everywhere.”

The fox stood beneath the tree and praised the crow’s beauty, saying that such a magnificent bird must surely have a beautiful voice as well. The crow, flattered by the compliment, opened its beak to sing and dropped the cheese straight into the fox’s waiting mouth.
